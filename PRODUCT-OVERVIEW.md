## What is ComplianceKit?

ComplianceKit is a comprehensive **GDPR Compliance SaaS Platform** that helps website owners, businesses, and organizations achieve and maintain compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other privacy regulations.

Think of ComplianceKit as your **automated compliance officer** - it scans your website, identifies compliance gaps, generates required legal documents, manages user consent, and helps you handle data subject requests - all from one centralized dashboard.

---

## The Problem We Solve

### Challenges Businesses Face:

1. **GDPR is Complex**: 99 articles, hundreds of pages of regulations, constantly evolving interpretations
2. **Legal Costs are High**: Hiring lawyers to draft privacy policies costs $5,000-$15,000+
3. **Technical Implementation is Difficult**: Cookie consent banners, data export systems, DSAR workflows
4. **Compliance is Time-Consuming**: Manual audits, tracking consent, responding to data requests
5. **Non-Compliance is Expensive**: GDPR fines up to ‚Ç¨20 million or 4% of global revenue
6. **Ongoing Maintenance**: Regulations change, new tracking scripts get added, policies need updates

### Our Solution:

ComplianceKit automates 90% of GDPR compliance work, reduces compliance costs by 80%, and ensures your website stays compliant with ongoing monitoring and updates.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Add Your Website
Sign up and add your website URL to ComplianceKit.

### Step 2: Run Initial Scan
ComplianceKit automatically scans your website to:
- Detect all cookies and tracking scripts
- Analyze your privacy policy (if you have one)
- Check your consent banner quality
- Identify compliance gaps
- Generate compliance score (0-100)

**Scan results show:**
- Overall compliance score
- Privacy policy completeness (0-100)
- Consent banner quality (0-100)
- List of all cookies detected
- List of all tracking scripts
- Detailed findings (errors, warnings, info)
- Specific recommendations

### Step 3: Generate Legal Documents
Based on your scan, generate:
- Privacy Policy (customized to your business)
- Cookie Policy (listing all detected cookies)
- Terms of Service

Download and add these to your website.

### Step 4: Install Consent Banner
Copy the embed code and add it to your website's `<head>` tag.

The banner will:
- Block non-essential cookies until consent is given
- Display granular consent options
- Track and store consent records
- Comply with all GDPR requirements

### Step 5: Set Up DSAR Widget
Add the DSAR submission form to your website so visitors can submit privacy requests directly to you.

### Step 6: Monitor & Maintain
ComplianceKit continuously:
- Re-scans your website periodically
- Alerts you to new compliance issues
- Tracks consent acceptance rates
- Manages DSAR requests
- Updates compliance score
